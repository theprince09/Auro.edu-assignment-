TIC TAC TOE - Friend vs Friend GAME RULES

  • Player 1 is X, Player 2 is O.
  • Players take turns marking a square.
  • The first to get 3 of their marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) wins.
  • If all 9 squares are filled and no player has 3 in a row, the game is a tie.
